How it works

01

Join as a founding PM to lead a new project.

You’re not just a PM. Not just a founder. You’re both. And we’re going to make sure you thrive.

02

Develop the product vision and roadmap.

We work with you to develop your product vision, economy design and roadmap.

03

Fund, launch and test.

We fund your seed round, provide development resources, expertise and infrastructure. You lead the building of the MVP.

04

Public launch and fundraise.

Launch to the market and we’ll help you raise an outside Series A from industry-leading crypto investors to fuel your future success.

05

Oh, the places you'll go.

Leave the SuperLayer studio post-Series A raise, but continue to leverage our team and resources as jet fuel for the entirety of your journey.
